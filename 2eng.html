The Green Bazaar

The Green Bazaar is one of the oldest markets in Almaty. It is located in the central part of the city, in the Medeu district. Shopping malls on the site of the Green Bazaar have existed since 1868.

Traveling trade - this trade was carried out by Russian, Tatar and Central Asian merchants. They sold their products to nomads
Fair trade - the most popular among cattle traders was the fair trade, which served as an intermediary between the pastoral and agricultural population.

Stationary trading - at the turn of the XIX-XX centuries. fair and traveling types of trade began to fall. Stationary trade was developed in the cities, which served as warehouses for factory products.
